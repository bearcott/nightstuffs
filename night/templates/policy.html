DMCA

We're sorry if we took your stuff! If you feel that your content has been published on this site without your permission, please take note that we (NightStuffs.com) will comply fully with a valid DMCA notice, please read before writing a DMCA notice:

  • Provide with the DMCA request, a verifiable email address and optionally a phone number.
  • Please write a valid DMCA notice to monkey@nightstuffs.com following the format below:
    • Provide reasonable proof that you are, or your company is, the copyright holder of the content.
    • Provide the exact URLs to the copyrighted content.
    • Provide the complete name(s) of the copyrighted content.
    • Read and include the following statement:
      I have a good faith belief that use of the copyrighted materials described above as allegedly infringing is not authorized by the copyright owner, its agent, or the law. I swear, under penalty of perjury, that the information in the notification is accurate and that I am the copyright owner or am authorized to act on behalf of the owner of an exclusive right that is allegedly infringed.
  • You will be liable for damages (including costs and attorneys' fees) for false DMCA notices.

Thank you! We will definitely get back to you after the above steps are considered.
